about

Hi. My name is Chris. I am from New Orleans. I graduated from Loyola University with a double major in Philosophy and Japanese. Then I went to China to study the board game Go with professional players. After that I spent three years in Miyazaki Prefecture in Japan teaching English to students ranging from kindergarten to junior high school. Since coming back to the states, I have been studying and teaching Go both online and in person. I also love cycling and reading. Nice to meet you.
